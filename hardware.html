    What is a motherboard and what does it do

    A motherboard is a very important part of a cmputer and it also is the
    part of the computer that helps communicate other important components.

    What is a CPU and what does it do?

    A CPU is a part of a computer that is a hardware and it sends out instructions
    to a computer program.

    What is a ram and what does it do?

    A ram is a computer version of a data storage. It stores data while your computer is on.
    Once you turn your computer off, the data in your ram will be deleted

    What is a hard drive and what does it do?

    A hard drive stores your data all of the time. The data doesn't disappear like ram,
    it actually stays saved in the computer.

    How do the different components communicate with one another?

    Different components communicate with one another because they are all connected
    to the motherboard because they are all in the motherboard and it releases signals
    to every piece of the computer.
